Project 2: HyperNarrative

Final: Lily Holic

This project is based on a movie All About Lily Chou-Chou. I incorporated my feelings and thoughts into this website. It combines the forms of video, forums and dialogues to tell the several moments in the original story where Philia shares his spiritual world(Ether) with others through Lily's songs. I chose the classic metaphor for ether from the movie, which is "Breath is blue Ether, and Erotica is red Ether". The choice of different Ether colors points to the plot of how the protagonist Philia brings the corresponding song to others, and they listened to the song together.

Vibe Coding Exercises

Vibe Coding Exercise 1

This exercise was inspired by the exhibition work "Can Poetry Be Written By Machines?" by artist Sasha Stiles. In this work. I want to create an web allowing users to reflect on how the interaction between people and poetry affects the meaning of poetry itself. When the poetry is displayed, it is in a relatively formal san-serif font. However, when the user places the mouse over it, the font of the poetry near the mouse will turn into handwritten script font, giving them artificial traces.


Vibe Coding Exercise 2

This exercise was inspired by the reading article "What Screens Want", which mentioned that early Internet aesthetics tended to simulate the texture and form in reality, like notes, calendar, etc. I created a collective workbench, bringing together some early real-life material style examples that I could think of and those listed in the article to form a relatively cohesive style.


Vibe Coding Exercise 3

This exercise is inspired by Laurel Schwulst. I created this UI based on her ideas about reducing excessive information on web pages and arranging websites in the form of a bookshelf.I directly used two rows of books to simulate a bookshelf way of information arrangement, allowing people to enter the internal cards through simple external information to create an information hierarchy


Vibe Coding Exercise 4

Inspired by 100 Anti-Theses in Cyberfeminism Index. I have borrowed the 100 anti-thesis and make them into a player animation. I mimic the aesthetic of the original site with blue background and white text and few decorative elements.


Vibe Coding Exercise 5

This is inspired by DJ_Dave. I wish to make a digital dj panel, which you could use multiple index to control the output of sound. Clicking the start button to play the sound and try to adjust it using track select and other options.
